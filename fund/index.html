Why a dollar

Small enough to give. Big enough to matter.

Plenty of young athletes have the discipline and the drive but not the budget — for mat time, a proper kit, or the fee to enter a competition. A single dollar feels like nothing on its own. But a dollar from a lot of people, together, can cover a whole season for a kid who would otherwise sit on the sidelines. This isn't about one person. It's about keeping talented athletes in the game.

Where your dollar goes

Every cent, straight to the mat.

Mat & gym time

Training sessions for athletes who can't cover their monthly fees.

Gear

A rashguard, a gi, gloves, a pair of shoes — the basics to train safely.

Competition fees

Registration and travel so athletes can actually step on and compete.
